French-Style Omelette (Stovetop)

Classic French-Style Omelette

A simple yet elegant dish, this French-style omelette is made with just 2 eggs, a pinch of salt and pepper, and a touch of milk or cream for tenderness.You can customize it with ingredients like cheese. Cooked gently in a buttered nonstick skillet, the omelette is softly set with a golden exterior and a creamy interior. Folded in half and served immediately, it’s a delicious, protein-rich meal perfect for any time of day. Quick, fluffy, and full of flavor — classic comfort food at its finest.

Ingredients for 1 person

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon milk or cream
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon grated cheese

Steps for prepare omelette

  1. Whisk the eggs – Crack 2 eggs into a bowl and add a pinch of salt, pepper, and (optional) 1 teaspoon of milk or cream.
  3. Beat until smooth – Whisk the mixture thoroughly until it becomes uniform and slightly frothy for a fluffy texture.
  4. Heat the skillet – Place a nonstick skillet (about 20 cm) on the stove over medium heat and add 1 teaspoon of butter, swirling to coat the pan.
  5. Pour in the egg mixture – Once the butter is melted and hot (but not browned), pour in the eggs and let them settle for a few seconds.
  6. Cook gently – As the edges begin to set, lift them slightly with a spatula and tilt the pan to allow the uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  7. Fold the omelette – When the top is still slightly runny but the bottom is golden, use the spatula to fold the omelette in half.
  8. Finish cooking – Cook for another 30–60 seconds, until the inside is softly set but still tender.
  9. Transfer to a plate – Carefully slide the omelette onto a warm serving plate.
  10. Serve immediately – Garnish with fresh herbs or serve with a side salad for a complete meal.
